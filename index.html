Sobre Nós

Na Security Analytics Company nós oferecemos uma abordagem fundamentalmente diferente à segurança corporativa. Nós temos consciência do cenário global de ameaças, do ponto de extremidade, à nuvem, ao datacenter, permitindo uma arquitetura de segurança holística.

Solutions

Whether you’re in the cloud or on premise, securing your applications, IoT devices, and sensitive data has become imperative to business operations.

Our approach to building an enterprise security model doesn’t begin with a specific product. We work to understand your business and assess your unique risk model. From there we assist in designing a security architecture that is both feasible and manageable with your current resources.
